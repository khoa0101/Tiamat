Tutorial

Glossary

  • Health: Health determines how tough the character is. The more health it has, the more hit it can take. If health reaches 0, the character is dead.
  • Power: Power determines how strong/powerful the character is. The more power the character has, the more damage it will deal to its enemies.
  • Armor: Armor determines how much damage the character can mitigate before taking damage to its health
  • Barrier: Like armor, barrier grants a temporary shield to the character. Barrier functions like an extra health bar. Barrier must be destroy before any damage can be dealt to target. Excess healing will grant barrier. (Note: Barrier will decay overtime).
  • Action Point(AP): Action point determines how many actions a character can make before ending its turn. Each actions(attack, abilities, spells, etc...) has different amount of action point cost.
  • Critical chance: Critical chance determines how accurate the character is at targeting the weak point of its enemy. Landing a critical hit increases the damage of an attack.
  • Critial damage: Critical damage determines how powerful the critial hit of an attack is.
  • Resistance: Resistance(physical, fire, water, lightning, poison, holy, dark, energy) determines how much damage the character will take upon receiving an attack. Having negative resistance will make an attack do more damage. Having resistance above 100% will heal the character instead.
  • Damage bonus: Damage bonus(physical, fire, water, lightning, poison, holy, dark, energy) determines how much more damage the character will deal to its target.
  • Lifesteal: Lifesteal heals the character performing the attack based on the damage dealt.
  • Cooldown: Cooldown indicates how many turns it takes before an ability can be used again.

Combat

  • Each character will start out with a set number of AP, and will recover some points after ending its turn. (Note:Any points left unused will be stored for the next round. Any points gain over the maximum is forfeit.)
  • You can left-click on any character or ability to see its details.
  • If it is the character's turn, the abilities will lit up. A lit up ability indicates it can be used (i.e. having enough AP and not on cooldown).
  • Left-click on an ability will prompt the player to select a target. Selecting a target will consume AP and put the ability on cooldown. (Note: During target selection, right-click anywhere to cancel the ability.)
  • Win by depleting all of your enemies' health.
  • Lose by depleting all of your team's health.
